Our Thinking

Providing system development and consulting service, our company was established from the four core values.

1. SENSE OF SERVICE

Development of IT system, consistent with our thought, is a service industry. With this meaning, our work is not different with the one at hotel or restaurant. To make easy-understanding proposals by whole mind then to implement them under strigent quality control is unfailingly thinking of all our staffs to serve customers satisfactorily.

2. CURIOSITY AND INITIATIVE

In the field of IT system development, we consider that surprise, discovery and inspiration are the most important things. Our staffs give into curiosity, take interested in every projects that they carry out.We aim to invest considerable budget in free newest technology research and development.

3. INTERNATIONALIZATION

As in Japan, globalization has been carried out on many fields and aspects. In our company, to create a product which applied world-wide informations, newest technologies in we have utilized English language in about half of documentations and softwares. Therefore, all of our staffs have propositional competence to comprehend English. Moreover, we trends to develop systems also for America, European, Asian countries ect. positively.

4. OFFERINGS OF ABUNDANCE

Bringing abundance to everybody through our business is also important, we think. Above having abundance of money or economic purely, our company operates with the hope of offerring you abundance in spirit and pleasure.
